Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept payments via PayPal, bank transfer, and mobile money for local clients. A 50% deposit is required to begin work on any project, with the balance due upon completion.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A basic website takes 2-3 weeks, while complex web applications may require 2-3 months. Design projects typically take 1-2 weeks. Exact timelines will be provided in your project proposal.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, I offer monthly maintenance plans starting at $99/month. These include updates, backups, security monitoring, and minor content changes. Custom maintenance plans are also available.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your package at any time. Any payments you've already made will be credited toward your upgraded package.
